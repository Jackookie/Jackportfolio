Project Overview

A top-down 2D game where the player can move in 8 directions and enter buildings such as houses.

Features

  • Player can move in 8 directions (WASD)
  • Top down perspective
  • Enter and leave buildings (houses)
  • Player Prefabs
  • Background music and sound effects(Door Open)

Technologies Used

Unity 6, C#
